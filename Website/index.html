Game Start

In the beginning everyone has to select an username. Afterwords one has to choose between hosting a new game or joining an existing game. In the lobby one has to select a character and to set the ready flag. Once everyone is ready, the host is able to assign the teams and to start the game. This action moves all players into the arena. It's all about shooting more goals than the enemy team. One can win through getting 11 goals before the timebank is gone or through having more goals when there is no time left.

Characters

Character Image 1

Erosion

  • highly movable plasma arms
  • control over ice and ball speed
Ice Slow
  • activates an ice effect on the shield
  • transmits the effect on the ball on hit
  • slows the next player that is hit by the ball
Power Bash
  • empowers the shield with a bash effect for a few seconds
  • applies the maximum speed to the ball when it hits the shield while the effect is active

Trashbot

  • trashcan rolling on two wheels
  • control over temporary walls and support bots
Character Image 1
Wall
  • allows the player to place a wall inside the arena
  • the wall blocks the ball and players
  • it gets split into pieces after a few seconds
Robot Army
  • shoots some small robots into the look direction of the player
  • the robots move forward until they hit something
  • each robot gets despawned on hit and slows players when they get hit

Powerups

Mine
  • the player gets a crosshair for aiming at the mine target
  • when thrown the mine flies towards the target and changes the direction when the target is moving
  • the mine gets attached to the target when it is reached
  • it explodes either on activation through the player or after a few seconds
Enlarge Shield
  • enlarges the player shield for a few seconds
  • this makes it easier to hit the ball
Speed Boost
  • increases the movement speed of the player for some time
  • it helps the player move faster and for example help the team mate
